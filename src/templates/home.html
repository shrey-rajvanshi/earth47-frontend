HOW IT WORKS

Select projects

Our team selects highest quality CO2 reducing projects from across the globe. Projects range from biogas to more efficient cookstoves to afforestation and more.

Verify impact

Each project has to meet a rigorous standard to ensure there is a measurable reduction in CO2. Verification reports are available on each project's page.

Crowdfund to reduce emissions

Your contribution offsets your carbon footprint and helps the projects remain financially viable. 90% of each contribution goes directly to the project.
